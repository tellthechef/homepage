HOW IT WORKS

here's the clever stuff

  • TellTheChef sends automatic personalised emails from your venue to your customers with links to online menus.
  • You simply log in and add the event details into TellTheChef. The email is sent and that’s your bit done... back to work!
  • The host then receives an email link to set up their event and invite their guests to place their choices online.
  • TellTheChef cleverly gathers orders through email invites, gently nudging guests to pre-order.
  • All events, guests and their pre-orders on one screen with place-cards, prep-lists, and reports at the touch of a button.
  • Branding and content control allows TellTheChef to slip into the background. Your guests won’t even know we're there.
  • Say hello to… more customer data, increased GP, lower staff costs, and better service.
